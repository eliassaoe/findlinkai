Frequently asked questions
Everything you need to know about the LinkedIn Search by Email API.
What types of email addresses work?
The API works best on professional business emails (e.g. john@company.com). Personal email addresses like Gmail or Outlook also work but have a lower match rate since they're less commonly listed on LinkedIn profiles.
What's the match rate?
For professional business emails the match rate is around 85-95%. For personal emails it drops to 50-70% depending on how publicly the person has associated that email with their LinkedIn profile.
How many credits does it cost?
1 credit per lookup regardless of whether a profile is found or not. Credits are included in all plans — Starter (60,000/yr), Professional (240,000/yr), Enterprise (600,000/yr).
Can I run this in bulk?
Yes. You can upload a CSV of emails directly in the app for bulk processing, or loop through your list via API. For large volumes (10,000+/month) the Professional or Enterprise plan gives the best per-credit rate.
What if no profile is found?
The API returns {"status": "not_found"} with an empty result field. You are not charged a credit if no match is found.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — sign up for free and get credits to test the API before committing to a plan. No credit card required to start.
