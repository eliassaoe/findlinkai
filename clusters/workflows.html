B2B Automation Workflows

Pre-built, repeatable workflows that turn raw signals — a company name, a LinkedIn post, a job listing — into enriched, outreach-ready lead lists. No manual research, no copy-paste.

An automation workflow chains several data operations into a single repeatable process. Instead of finding a company, then hunting for employees, then enriching each contact with an email and phone number by hand, a workflow does the whole sequence for you and hands back a clean, structured list. It's the difference between an afternoon of tab-switching and a job that runs while you do something else.

The workflows below are built for sales, marketing, and recruiting teams who already know who they want to reach but don't want to assemble the data manually. Each one starts from an input you already have — company names, a LinkedIn post URL, a job posting — and ends with enriched records you can push straight into your CRM or outreach tool. They run on the same enrichment engine behind our Data Enrichment API, so the output quality matches our standalone tools.

Pick a workflow that matches the signal you're working from. If none fit exactly, most can be adapted, or you can combine steps using the API directly.
