LinkedIn Data, Scraping & APIs

Everything we build for extracting and enriching LinkedIn data — profile and company scrapers, developer APIs, email and phone finders, and tools for turning posts and engagement into leads.

LinkedIn is the richest source of B2B contact data on the web, but it's locked behind a UI that isn't built for collecting data at scale. The tools below close that gap: they pull structured information — names, titles, companies, emails, phone numbers, post engagement — from LinkedIn profiles, companies, and posts, and hand it back in a clean, usable format.

Whether you need a one-off lookup, a bulk export, or a developer API to wire LinkedIn data into your own product, there's a tool here for it. They all run on the same engine behind our Data Enrichment API, so accuracy stays consistent across every use case. Pick the category that matches what you're trying to do.
