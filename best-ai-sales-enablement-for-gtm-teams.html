Sales managers get visibility into deal risk, coaching opportunities, and pipeline health. Reps receive real-time guidance during calls and post-call insights to improve performance. While powerful, Gong requires significant investment and works best for larger sales teams with high deal values.

AI-Powered CRM Intelligence Built into Salesforce

Salesforce Einstein is an AI layer built directly into the Salesforce CRM, offering predictive lead scoring, opportunity insights, and automated data entry. For teams already using Salesforce, Einstein provides native AI capabilities without switching platforms.

Einstein analyzes your historical CRM data to predict which leads will convert, which deals are likely to close, and what actions reps should take next. It automatically captures emails and meeting notes, scores leads based on fit and engagement, and surfaces key insights within your existing Salesforce interface. The challenge is that Einstein's effectiveness depends heavily on data quality and CRM hygiene, and many advanced features require expensive add-ons beyond the base price.
