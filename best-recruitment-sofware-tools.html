PhantomBuster

LinkedIn Automation Tool for Candidate Outreach

PhantomBuster is a LinkedIn automation platform that helps recruiters extract candidate data and automate outreach at scale. Unlike the ATS platforms above, PhantomBuster focuses specifically on LinkedIn scraping and automation, making it popular with sourcers and recruiters who do high-volume candidate outreach.

The tool offers pre-built "Phantoms" that can scrape LinkedIn search results, export profile data, send connection requests, and message candidates automatically. However, PhantomBuster operates through your LinkedIn account, which means you risk account restrictions if LinkedIn detects automation. The platform limits you to 2 hours of scraping per day to reduce ban risk, but this doesn't eliminate it entirely.

LinkedIn Recruiter

Official LinkedIn Sourcing and Outreach Platform

LinkedIn Recruiter is LinkedIn's official recruiting platform that provides advanced search capabilities, InMail credits, and pipeline management tools. Unlike third-party scraping tools, LinkedIn Recruiter is completely safe to use since it's a first-party LinkedIn product with no ban risk.

The platform gives you access to LinkedIn's full database of 900+ million professionals with sophisticated filters for skills, experience, location, and more.
