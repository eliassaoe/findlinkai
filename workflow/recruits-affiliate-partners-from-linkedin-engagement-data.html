About this workflow

Finding and recruiting quality affiliate partners is one of the most time-consuming tasks for partnership and affiliate marketing teams. Manually scrolling through LinkedIn posts, identifying potential influencers, researching their reach, and hunting down contact information can take hours‚Äîoften with inconsistent results and missed opportunities.

This workflow transforms affiliate recruitment by automatically mining LinkedIn post reactions for high-potential partners. Simply provide a viral post URL from your niche, and the workflow extracts every person who engaged, enriches their profiles with follower counts and company data, calculates an influence score based on reach and relevance, and retrieves verified company emails‚Äîall exported directly to Google Sheets.

With built-in filtering for minimum follower thresholds and intelligent priority ranking (high, medium, low), you'll instantly know which prospects deserve immediate outreach. Process 50-200+ profiles per post automatically, turning hours of manual research into minutes of automated intelligence gathering.

Step-by-step guide

Set up this workflow in minutes with these simple steps

1

Get your LinkFinder AI API key

Sign up at linkfinderai.com and get your API key. You'll need this to enable all enrichment services including post reactions, profile data, and company email lookup.

2

Import the workflow to n8n

Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance. Replace YOUR_API_KEY_HERE in all three LinkFinder AI nodes with your actual API key.

3

Configure your input settings

In the "Input - Post URL & Settings" node, paste a LinkedIn post URL from your niche (ideally viral content), set your minimum follower threshold, and customize target keywords for headline matching.

4

Connect Google Sheets

Authenticate your Google account and either create a new spreadsheet or connect to an existing one. The workflow will automatically append enriched influencer data with all contact details and scores.

5

Run and review your influencer database

Execute the workflow to process all post reactions. Access your Google Sheet with prioritized influencers including influence scores, verified emails, and ready-for-outreach flags for immediate recruitment.
