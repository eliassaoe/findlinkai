About this workflow

Get truly qualified, engaged leads based on hiring signals. This n8n workflow builds your AI-powered lead qualification system that monitors job postings to identify companies actively hiring key decision-makers.

It automatically processes job posting data, enriches each company with full business intelligence from multiple sources, calculates an intent score based on role seniority and company fit, and outputs a prioritized lead list ready for your sales team.

Scalability: Processes unlimited job postings in batches of 10, handling everything from small daily lists to large market scans.

Step-by-step guide

Set up this workflow in minutes with these simple steps

1

Get your LinkFinder AI API key

Sign up at linkfinderai.com and get your API key. You'll need this to enable all enrichment services.

2

Import the workflow to n8n

Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance. Replace YOUR_API_KEY_HERE in all LinkFinder AI nodes with your actual key.

3

Configure your input data

Edit the "Input - Job Postings List" node with your actual job posting data, or connect it to an external source like Apify webhook.

4

Test and activate

Run a test with sample data to verify the enrichment pipeline. Once validated, activate the workflow and connect to your CRM.

5

Review enriched leads

Access your prioritized lead list with complete company data, intent scores, and actionable insights for your sales team.
