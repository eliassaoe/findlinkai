About this workflow

Building comprehensive account maps and organizational charts manually is incredibly time-consuming. Sales teams, recruiters, and researchers often spend hours navigating LinkedIn to understand company structures, identify decision makers, and gather contact information for their target accounts.

This workflow automates the entire company intelligence gathering process. Simply add company names to a Google Sheet, and the workflow automatically finds their LinkedIn profiles, extracts complete company details, identifies all employees, filters for decision makers (CEOs, Owners, Chiefs, Chairmen), and enriches the data with company phone numbers and emails.

Built for scalability, the workflow processes companies in batches of 5 and can handle up to 20 companies per run. Each company profile includes name, description, location, website, company size, specialties, industry, plus leadership contact details‚Äîeverything you need for effective account-based outreach.

Step-by-step guide

Set up this workflow in minutes with these simple steps

1

Get your LinkFinder AI API key

Sign up at linkfinderai.com and get your API key. You'll need this to enable all enrichment services including company lookup, employee extraction, and contact finding.

2

Import the workflow to n8n

Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance. Replace ENTER_API_KEY in all 5 LinkFinder AI nodes with your actual API key.

3

Connect your Google Sheet

Create a Google Sheet with a column for company names. Connect the Google Sheets Trigger node to your sheet and configure the OAuth credentials for automatic monitoring.

4

Test and activate

Add a few test company names to your sheet and run the workflow manually. Verify the org chart data is being extracted correctly, then activate the workflow for automatic processing.

5

Review your org charts

Access your aggregated results containing complete company profiles with decision maker contacts, company details, and organizational intelligence ready for your CRM or outreach tools.
