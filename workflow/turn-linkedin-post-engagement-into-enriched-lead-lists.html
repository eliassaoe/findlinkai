About this workflow

Finding qualified leads who are already interested in your industry is one of the biggest challenges in B2B sales. Cold outreach to random prospects yields low response rates, but what if you could target people who have already shown interest in relevant content? That's exactly what this workflow solves.

This automation takes any LinkedIn post URL‚Äîwhether it's from a competitor, industry influencer, or trending topic‚Äîand extracts every single person who engaged with it. Each profile is then enriched through LinkFinder AI to add verified email addresses, phone numbers, job titles, company names, and locations. The result is a complete lead list of warm prospects who care about your topic.

The workflow processes engagements in batches of 5 to ensure reliable API performance, filters out profiles without email addresses, and exports everything to Google Sheets for easy CRM import. Posts with 1,000+ reactions can generate 1,000+ pre-qualified leads in a single run, making this perfect for sales teams looking to scale their outreach with warm, targeted prospects.

Step-by-step guide

Set up this workflow in minutes with these simple steps

1

Get your LinkFinder AI API key

Sign up at linkfinderai.com and get your API key. You'll need this to enable the post reaction scraping and profile enrichment services.

2

Import the workflow to n8n

Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance. Replace ENTER_API_KEY in both LinkFinder AI HTTP nodes with your actual API key.

3

Configure your Google Sheet

Connect your Google Sheets account and create a new spreadsheet with columns: name, email, phone, job, company, linkedin url, location. Update the Google Sheets node with your spreadsheet ID.

4

Test with a LinkedIn post

Find a LinkedIn post with high engagement in your target industry. Copy the post URL, trigger the workflow form, and paste the URL to run a test. Verify leads appear in your Google Sheet.

5

Scale and export your leads

Run the workflow on multiple viral posts to build a massive lead database. Export from Google Sheets to your CRM and start outreach to prospects who are already warm to your topic.
